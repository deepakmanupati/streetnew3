Transparent Digital Signage is a innovative technology by NEXNOVO allowing you to turn your store glass window into a digital billboard, suitable for advertising all products and services. The integrated LED lamps are almost invisible from the outside of the building, thus making no difference to its appearance. This type of digital signage provides 80% transparency which enables you to still be able to look through the glass window.
